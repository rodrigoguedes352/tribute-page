Elon Musk

Some of the things Elon Musk has done in his life-time

  • 28th of june 1971 - The year in which Elon Musk was born.
  • 1995 - Elon musk and his brother created his first company, called Zip2 (sold in 1999 for $307 Million).
  • 1997 - Elon musk graduated from college earning two bachelor's degrees in physics and economics.
  • 1997 - Elon musk created X.com an online bank.
  • 2000 - Elon musk merged with Confinity to create PayPal.
  • 2002 - PayPal was sold to eBay for $1.5 Billion, in the same year before the deal was made, Elon musk founded SpaceX, he and his brother also created the Musk Foundation
  • 2004 - Elon musk joins Tesla as one of its chairman.
  • 2007 - Elon musk becomes CEO of Tesla and Lead product Architect.
  • 2016 - Tesla purchases SolarCity and becomes one of the biggest solar panels installer in the US. Elon also founded The boring company and Neuralink.
